The Best Movies According to Reyansh

My top 3 movies of all-time

We're the Millers

A small-time drug dealer creates a fake family to smuggle marijuana across the Mexican border, and the plan spirals into chaos.

The Hangover(all three parts)

A group of friends repeatedly wake up from outrageous bachelor-party disasters with no memory of the night before and must unravel the chaos they caused.

Anyone But You

Two people who can't stand each other pretend to be a couple at a destination wedding, only to find their fake romance getting complicated.
